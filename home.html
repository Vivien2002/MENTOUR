Frustrated About College?

We want to help! Mentour offers free volunteer suggestions, college search help and a calender filled with popular college information sessions and tours. Here at Mentour we are dedicated to our students and want to make sure every student succeeds! Come and sign up today...


Our Volunteer Opportunities

We want to make sure that all our students are able to experience their career path with a plethora of volunteer options. We offfer great volunteer services for every major and career.


The Colleges We provide Information About

We offer information about a great a variety of popular colleges in New York State.This website is meant for users in the state and those who want to maybe experience New York. Those who want to learn more about the education offered in this wonderful state.



About Us?

We are a dedicated group of student creators that want our peers to have the same resources we did. We want all our peers to be succcessful and happy with their college decisions.
